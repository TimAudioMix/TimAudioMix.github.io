About

Who is TimAudioMix?

I’m Timofey, musician and artist performing in China and Europe, and a mixing engineer helping artists shape their sound. My background is in classical performance (bayan, accordion, bandoneon) and live shows, which gives me a strong feel for dynamics, groove and emotion in every track.

I work in a compact but accurate setup, focusing on clarity, punch and musicality rather than just loudness. Whether it’s a club track, pop single or live acoustic recording – the goal is always the same: make your music feel expensive and emotional.
